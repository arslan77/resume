\vspace{2mm}

\begin{cvparagraph}
Developed a computer vision system using transfer learning to automatically extract socioeconomic indicators from high-resolution satellite imagery. The system generates poverty and development heat maps by identifying geographical and man-made features (highways, buildings, farmlands) from publicly available satellite data, achieving comparable accuracy to traditional field surveys while being significantly more cost-effective and scalable for developing countries.
\end{cvparagraph}
